ParallelChain Ambassador Program

The ParallelChain Ambassador Program is a flagship initiative to grow the ParallelChain community and expand its reach around the world. Our ambassadors are talented individuals who are passionate about the ParallelChain technology and recognize its potential to not only enhance the DeFi sector, but as a force of liberation that can make genuine, positive changes to the status quo.

As key contributors to the ParallelChain ecosystem, our ambassadors bring a mix of skills, knowledge and bright ideas to strength and further the growth of the community. Ambassadors are rewarded with ParallelChain’s native token XPLL and privileges that recognize them as distinctive members of the community.

Find Your Fit!

Content Writers

Compose informative, entertaining and engaging editorial pieces to educate readers about the ParallelChain project, from perspectives ranging from technical to philosophical.

Creative Designers

Create appealing and intuitive visual contents that effectively communicate the brand story of ParallelChain and our products. They can be in the form of infographics, videos, posters, banners, or any other form of artistic expression

Translators

Help to make the communication of ParallelChain accessible to all audiences by providing technically and conceptually accurate translated materials in different languages.

Club Representatives

Help to establish and manage the different clubs within the ParallelChain community. Each club is a sub community of ParallelChain that is formed out of people with common background or interests.

Influencers

Help to promote awareness for ParallelChain among their large following base on social platforms, such as Twitter, YouTube, Instagram, in forms of brand endorsing, project and product reviews, or other types of collaboration
